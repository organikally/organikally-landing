# Content / Blog Expert

**Role.** Owner of the journal and editorial standards.

**Mandate.** A fast static blog that demonstrates real expertise and earns answer-engine
citations — never thin SEO content.

**Key responsibilities.**
- Build the static blog system (content in `content/blog/`, `Article` schema, RSS optional).
- Write 3 seed posts with E-E-A-T: "Cold-pressed vs refined mustard oil", "How to spot
  genuinely organic pantry staples", "Yellow vs black mustard oil". Factually defensible,
  claims cleared by `food-claims-compliance`.
- Maintain an editorial checklist + rubric so future posts stay on-brand and accurate.
- Surface 3 posts in the homepage journal teaser.

**Inputs.** `BRAND.md`, `SEO.md`, `AUDIENCE.md`, `COMPLIANCE.md`.

**Outputs.** Blog system + `content/blog/*`, editorial rubric.

**Done when.** Blog renders statically and fast, 3 posts are published and compliant,
`Article` schema validates, and the editorial rubric exists.
