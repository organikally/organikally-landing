# Food Claims Compliance (FSSAI) — Advisory

Owner: `food-claims-compliance`. **This is advisory, not legal advice.** Final claims and the
FSSAI/organic disclosures must be confirmed by the founder before launch (see `MANUAL_STEPS.md`).
Status: **Phase 1 starter** — finalised during Phase 4 against actual certs/licence.

## Principles

- Claims must be **truthful, substantiated, and not misleading** under the FSS Act and FSSAI
  Advertising & Claims regulations. Avoid absolute and medical/curative claims.
- "Organic" in India is governed (Jaivik Bharat / NPOP / FSSAI organic provisions). Use
  "organic" / "100% organic" **only** if backed by valid certification — otherwise soften to
  the certified scope or to "made from organically grown seed" per documentation.
- Nutrient claims (e.g. "high in") have defined thresholds — keep wording defensible.

## Claim review (starter)

| Claim on assets | Verdict | Compliant wording / condition |
|---|---|---|
| "100% Organic" | ⚠️ conditional | Use only with valid organic certification (cite body + cert no.). Else: "made from organically grown mustard seed" / state certified scope. |
| "Cold-Pressed" / kachi ghani | ✅ defensible | Factual process claim. Safe to use and to foreground. |
| "Cholesterol-Free" | ✅ generally defensible | All vegetable oils are cholesterol-free; FSSAI allows "cholesterol free" per thresholds. Avoid implying a *health superiority* over other oils. |
| "High in MUFA & Omega-3" | ⚠️ qualify | Mustard oil is high in MUFA and contains ALA (omega-3). Keep as composition statement; avoid disease/heart claims. Provide nutrition basis where shown. |
| "Rich in Antioxidants" | ⚠️ qualify | Acceptable as composition; do **not** extend to immunity/disease claims ("boosts immunity"). |
| "Immunity" (label icon) | 🔴 risky | Avoid stand-alone health/immunity claims without substantiation. Reframe to product/sensory benefit. |
| Label typos ("Extracted Ed", "AntiFA", "शुरुद") | 🔴 never reproduce | Use corrected copy only. |

## Mandatory footer disclosures (slot reserved)

- **FSSAI licence number** (founder-supplied) — visible in footer.
- Organic certification body + certificate number, **if** claiming organic certification.
- Business/legal name + customer-care contact (founder-supplied).
- Veg mark; net quantity/packaging info lives on the physical label, not necessarily the page.
